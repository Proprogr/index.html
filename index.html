What it means to be and living as a differently-able [disabled] person ?
In addition to teaching activities there are also volunteering, sensibilisations about disability and vulnerability as well as drumming.
Learn More...
Sometimes they are considered to be the most courageous and angellustic people with a strong character by the ones and get included. But sometimes they are ridiculed, deviled, physically assaulted [Women with disabilities are more exposed to sexual harrasments]. They are experiencing many kinds of discriminations and then get excluded by the others. It should'nt. The people never lack words and ways to cheer them or drive fear to them. When it comes to include them by helping them have opportunities, that same people has sometimes been absent, indifferent or/and reticent about pushing and promoting inclusion in the communities. But disabled people are also human-beings and differently able who fairly deserve their rights. The right to get a fair care, the right to study, the right at food safety, the right to get a job and the right to found a family if the conditions are gathered. Given a stammering inclusionary culture, those people have to struggle two times harder than any else to meagerly get their rights. Even few extreme cases where some people sucidiarily behaved because a culminated distress, many of them never give up. Some are activating for their rights that are cesaselessly violated. In a spite of multiple adversities, it's unclear without supports, they will be able to fulfill their dreams. That is why Tiki N'Yiki is founded in order to support them.

Etymology and Meaning
Tiki N'Yiki is etymologically structured of two (2) words of Moore, a language in Burkina Faso :

1. Tiki is litteraly meaning LEANING UPON

2. N'Yiki is meaning GETTING UP

Why Tiki N'Yiki ?
Tiki N'Yiki is created to reasonably support efforts of disabled people including disabled kids in Burkina Faso. It resonates as a slogan adressing a necessity for everyone to be aware of being the best archetect of his own sucess. Tiki N'Yiki stimulatively supports the idea that every disabled kid can have a better future if helped.
Learn More...
Region of Implementation
Tiki N'Yiki has first been implemented in Burkina Faso in 2016. Amongs many reasons is the main one that focuses to create a free and ideal space and time on improving education for disabled kids by many ways. In the future Tiki N'Yiki would be extanding to neighboring countries like Benin, Ghana as well Ivory Coast depending on positive and evoluting conditions of security in the region. Tiki N'Yiki necesarily targeting disabled and entrepreneurial communities and leaders who run projects, entreprises or/and businesses that help promote disability and inclusion in terms of how they could do to impact disabled communuties through the benefits the people are receiving.
