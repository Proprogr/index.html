Welcome on Tiki N'Yiki's Website !
This website is in project. It will be dedicated to improve education, promote inclusion and protect rights of disabled people. Tiki N'Yiki through its Vision, focuses especially on DISABLED CHILDREN, their INCLUSION and EDUCATION in Burkina Faso in order to HELP them realize their DREAMS. One of the main leverages of dreaming is fundamentally to getting EDUCATED. It is not onely a great pleasure to have you here and wanting to learn more about realities of being, living as a disabled person but also and hopefully you'll become a relay and help to enhance inclusion and defend the rights of disabled people in the society if needed. Thank you for your time and feedback !

Tiki N'Yiki | Etymology, Meaning, Reason and Region of Implementation
Etymology and Meaning

Tiki N'Yiki is etymologically structured of two (2) words of Moore, a language in Burkina Faso :

1. Tiki is litteraly meaning Leaning upon

2. N'Yiki is meaning Getting up
Learn More...
Why Tiki N'Yiki ?

Tiki N'Yiki is created to improve education and support efforts of disabled children in Burkina Faso. It is sounding as a slogan adressing the necessity and the possibility for everyone to lead his own achievement. Tiki N'Yiki stimulatively supports the idea that every disabled kid can have a better future if helped.
Learn More...
Region of Implementation

Tiki N'Yiki has first been implemented in Burkina Faso in 2016. Amongs many reasons is the main one that focuses to create a free and ideal space and time on improving education for disabled kids. In the future Tiki N'Yiki would be extanding to neighboring countries like Benin, as well Ivory Coast depending with good security conditions in the region.
